The Best Movies According to Fesseha

My top 3 movies of all time.

Coming to America

Coming to America" is a delightful journey filled with laughter and heart. Eddie Murphy's charm shines as he teaches us about the true meaning of love and acceptance.

The Hangover

The Hangover" delivers uproarious laughs with its talented cast. Cooper, Helms, and Galifianakis shine, creating comedic chaos that makes for an immensely enjoyable watch.

Titanic

"Titanic" brilliantly intertwines romance and tragedy amid the historical disaster. Stellar performances and captivating visuals make it a timeless cinematic masterpiece. Truly unforgettable.
